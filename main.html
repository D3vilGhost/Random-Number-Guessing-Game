Welcome to Random Number Guessing Game!

How To Play/Rules:

  • CPU will choose a Random Number between 1 & 100.
  • You have to guess the number choosen by CPU.
  • You have 10 attempts to guess the number.
  • If your entered number is greater than the number you have to guess, it will show you the following message:
    (Number is LOWER than the number you have entered!).
  • If your entered number is lower than the number you have to guess, it will show you the following message:
    (Number is GREATER than the number you have entered!).
  • Initially your score will be 10.
  • Based on the number of attemps you take to guess the number , your score will be decided.
